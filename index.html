Our Story

A True Irish Local

The Hurlers is one of those rare pubs that hasn't forgotten what a local should be. Housed in a beautifully kept thatched building, it's a place where regulars feel at home and newcomers quickly become regulars.

We take pride in great food made fresh every day, a warm welcome at every visit, and the kind of atmosphere you only find in a real Irish bar. Whether you're calling in for a quick pint or settling in for the evening — you're always welcome at The Hurlers.
